Welcome to Fyle

Fyle is your secure, offline document management solution. Scan, upload, categorize, and manage important documents like insurance papers, academic records, and vehicle documents—all on your device.

Version: 1.0
Released: 05 April 2025

Key Features

  • Scan and upload documents using your camera or file picker.
  • Extract key information with on-device OCR and machine learning.
  • Categorize documents and set reminders for expiry or renewal dates.
  • Share documents securely via system share sheet or peer-to-peer.
  • Works completely offline—no internet required.

Get Started

Download Fyle from the App Store and start managing your documents today. Your data stays private and local on your device.
